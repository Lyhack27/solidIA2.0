Privacy Policy

At solid AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you fill out our contact form (name, email, phone number, business problem). We also collect anonymous usage data to improve our services.

Use of Information

Your information is used to respond to your inquiries, provide requested services, and improve our website and AI solutions. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is entirely secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Please contact us to exercise these rights.
